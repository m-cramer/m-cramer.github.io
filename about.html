About Me

I remember it clearly—I was perched against a wall during first grade recess, writing down my latest ideas for the spiral-bound story I had been working on for that entire year. What had started as a measly assignment turned into a 100-page story with a mind of its own. Although I didn’t know it at the time, this was the start of my passion for writing.

Through the years, I continued to write, and when I reached high school, it took a journalistic turn. I joined my high school’s newspaper, where I became editor of the entertainment section. I learned the ins and outs of writing for a news publication, and it fueled my desire to continue this style of writing in college.

The little girl who spent all of her free time writing had no clue that it would take her to where she is today: studying Broadcast Journalism at the University of Miami. While in school, I hope to further practice my writing skills and become fully prepared for a career in the field.

Ideally, I would love to report on either sports or entertainment, and I certainly have a passion for both topics. I follow both religiously and can’t imagine my life without them.

I’m tenacious. I’m passionate. I go for what I want and don’t stop until I get it. I’m a writer through and through– always have been and always will be.
